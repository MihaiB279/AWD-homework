What Is Happiness?

Happiness is a state of well-being that includes positive emotions, life satisfaction, and a sense of purpose. It is not only about feeling good, but also about living a meaningful and balanced life.

Why Happiness Matters

Happiness has a strong impact on mental health, physical health, relationships, and productivity. Happy people tend to cope better with stress and enjoy a higher quality of life.
